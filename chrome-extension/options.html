RAKSHA AI Companion

Configure the portal this extension opens and controls.

Use the HTTPS address of your deployed RAKSHA frontend. For local development, http://localhost:3000 is supported.

Permissions and privacy

The companion only reads the active RAKSHA page after you use its voice button. The selected portal address is stored in Chrome Sync. Spoken commands are handled through Chrome’s speech-recognition service and are passed to the active RAKSHA page; the extension does not run its own analytics or send data to a separate server.
