# RAKSHA AI Companion privacy policy

Effective date: July 21, 2026

RAKSHA AI Companion lets users open and optionally control their configured RAKSHA AI portal from Chrome.

## Information handled

- **Portal address:** The extension stores the portal URL chosen by the user in Chrome Sync so it remains available across their signed-in Chrome browsers.
- **Voice commands:** When the user presses the microphone button, Chrome’s speech-recognition capability processes the spoken command. The recognised text is sent only to the active configured RAKSHA portal to carry out the requested navigation or form-fill action.
- **Active-page data:** The extension reads the active page only after the user invokes a voice command and only on the configured RAKSHA portal. It uses that access to pass the command to the page.

## Information not collected by the extension

The extension does not operate its own analytics, advertising, tracking, or separate data-collection server. It does not sell personal information.

## User choices

Voice control is optional. Users can deny microphone access and use all popup shortcuts without it. Users can change or remove the saved portal address from the extension settings, or uninstall the extension to remove its local extension data.

## Important note

The RAKSHA portal itself may process reports, account information, and other submitted content under its own privacy policy. Deployers must publish the RAKSHA portal’s privacy policy and configure the public contact address below before publishing this extension.
